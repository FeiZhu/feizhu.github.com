Personal Profile

I am a Ph.D. student at Graphics and Interactive Technology Lab of Peking University, advised by Prof. Guoping Wang. I joined the lab in 2010, after graduating from Xi'an Jiaotong University with a B.E. degree in Computer Science.

I do research in computer graphics, with primary focus on physics based animation. More specifically, I'm working on alogrithms for fast simulation and artistic control of complex deformable materials. I'm aslo interested and experienced in computational simulation of fracture, fluid, and hair. Here is my CV.
