Personal Profile

I am a Ph.D. student in Graphics and Interactive Technology Lab at Peking University, advised by Prof. Guoping Wang. I joined the lab in 2010, after graduating from Xi'an Jiaotong University with a B.E. degree in Computer Science.

I do research in computer graphics, with primary focus on physics based animation. Most recently, I am investigating methods for easy and intuitive control of physics based animation. Before that I tried to couple high resolution embedded surface mesh to low resolution Eulerian solid simulation in order to simulate fracture. For more information about my research experience, please see my CV.
